In this first section I have decided to put some images of paintings, most of them oil paintings, which convey a sense of search, lost, anguished and that's why I have also decided to put a poem that can go hand in hand with the images.

MY PERSONALITY

For the second block I am going to focus on photographs that could define me or that when you see them you think of me: as a person, as an artist, as a thought, etc.

That's all, I am the one that appears in the images -3-.

MUSIC BITS

I have chosen 3 songs of very similar styles and that fit with this web page. They are slow songs, with deep lyrics that deal with different themes in a delicate and subtle way thanks to the melody that composes them. I have decided to put a lyric video of the song “Video Game by ICELANDIA”.
